## 9. Risks & Considerations

### Brand Transition Risks
- **User Confusion:** Existing users may not recognize the new brand
  - *Mitigation:* Add transitional messaging explaining the rebrand
- **SEO Impact:** Brand name change may affect search rankings
  - *Mitigation:* Implement proper redirects and update meta tags
- **Market Positioning:** Premium positioning may alienate price-sensitive users
  - *Mitigation:* Keep entry price point accessible (â‚©3,900)

### Technical Risks
- **Browser Compatibility:** Advanced CSS effects may not work on older browsers
  - *Mitigation:* Progressive enhancement approach
- **Performance:** Heavy animations may slow load times
  - *Mitigation:* Lazy loading and optimized animations
- **Mobile Experience:** Complex grid layouts may break on small screens
  - *Mitigation:* Thorough responsive testing

### Business Risks
- **Strategy Center Adoption:** B2B feature may not gain traction
  - *Mitigation:* Plan targeted outreach to marketing professionals
- **Physical Studio Expectation Gap:** Digital service vs. physical locations may confuse users
  - *Mitigation:* Clear messaging about service model
